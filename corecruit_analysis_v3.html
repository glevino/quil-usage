Mark's Three Questions

Q1  |  How's the usage across the company?
Strong and growing. The team has logged 1,748 hours across 9.5 months with zero complete washout weekdays. Monthly usage scaled from 19 hrs in the ramp month (June) to a consistent 190 to 216 hrs/month by fall, and has held steady into 2026 at ~196 hrs/month. That's not a honeymoon effect. Across the team, 7,962 meetings were logged with a 76% connect rate.
Q2  |  Does almost everyone use this regularly?
Mostly yes, and strongly so. 16 of 20 users are genuinely active. Ten qualify as Power Users (5,000+ minutes), six are Regular users (2,000 to 4,999 min), one is a Light user, and three are effectively non-adopters. The median user has logged 4,970 minutes (~83 hrs) over the period. The core team is using this as a daily workflow tool, not occasionally.
Q3  |  Do we have any non-adopters?
Yes, three confirmed. Brendan Adinolfi (378 min, 22 calls), Bill Young (131 min, 10 calls), and Mark Peffer (94 min, 3 calls) are well below any meaningful usage threshold. Bill's number likely reflects his role as a non-frontline user. These three represent seats where per-seat ROI is minimal, but they don't materially drag on the overall value story given how strongly the rest of the team performs.
